Virtual tours have been stuck in the panorama era for too long. Stitched 360-degree photos feel flat, lifeless, and disconnected from the spaces they try to represent. We knew there had to be a better way.

## The Problem with Traditional Virtual Tours

Traditional virtual tour platforms rely on equirectangular panoramas — essentially flat images wrapped around a sphere. They work, but they strip away the depth and dimensionality that makes a space feel real.

You can look around, but you can't truly *explore*. The geometry is an illusion.

## Enter Gaussian Splatting

Gaussian splatting is a breakthrough in 3D scene reconstruction. Instead of meshes or point clouds, it represents scenes as collections of 3D Gaussians — volumetric elements that capture lighting, color, and transparency with photorealistic fidelity.

The result? Scenes that feel genuinely three-dimensional. You can orbit, zoom, and explore from angles the original photographer never captured.

## What CloudTour Offers

With CloudTour, you can:

- **Upload splat files** (.ply, .splat, .spz) captured from real-world scenes
- **Build multi-scene tours** with waypoints connecting different spaces
- **Add hotspots** with rich content — text, images, and more
- **Share publicly** with SEO-optimized pages and embeddable viewers
- **View in Apple Vision Pro** via WebXR for true spatial computing
